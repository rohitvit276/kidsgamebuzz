Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2, 2026

Kids Game Buzz ("we", "our") provides free games for young children through our website www.kidsgamebuzz.com and our Kids Game Buzz Android app. We built Kids Game Buzz for children, so we keep things as private as possible: we do not collect, store, sell, or share personal information from children or anyone else.

The Android App

The Kids Game Buzz Android app works completely offline and requests no permissions on your device. It does not:

  • collect or transmit any personal information;
  • use analytics, advertising, or tracking of any kind;
  • connect to the internet at all — every game is bundled inside the app;
  • contain ads, in-app purchases, external links, or social features;
  • require an account, sign-up, or login.

Everything that happens in the app (scores, drawings, game progress) stays on the device and disappears when the app is closed or uninstalled.

The Website

Our website does not require an account and does not ask visitors for any personal information. Like many websites, it uses Google Analytics to understand overall, anonymous usage — for example, how many people visited and which games are popular. Google Analytics may use cookies and collect standard technical information such as an approximate location (city level), browser type, and pages viewed. We do not use this data to identify anyone, and we never receive names, email addresses, or precise locations. You can learn how Google handles this data at policies.google.com/privacy and opt out with the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on.

Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Our games are designed for children ages 2–7. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar laws worldwide by simply not collecting personal information from anyone, including children. We do not knowingly collect, use, or disclose any personal data from children. Because the Android app is fully offline with no data collection whatsoever, there is nothing for a parent to review or delete — but if you have any concern, please contact us and we will help.

Third Parties

The Android app contains no third-party services. The website uses only Google Analytics (described above) and Google Fonts to display text. We do not sell or share any data with anyone.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change how the app or website works in a way that affects privacy, we will update this page and the "Last updated" date above before the change takes effect.
